Automatic extraction

A Supabase Edge Function processes each document through the Claude API (Sonnet). It extracts key fields and saves them to the database automatically.

🤖

Claude Sonnet 4

Processes PDFs and images with vision. Extracts structured JSON for each document type.

🔌

4 extraction templates

Governance, Org Chart, IDs, Bank Statement. Each with specific field prompts.

⚡

Parallel processing

All documents extract concurrently. Results merge into the application form.

👥 Agregar Personas

Upload your people

This is where the company onboards their remote workers, freelancers, and creators. Two methods: add them one by one manually, or bulk upload via CSV.

📝

Manual entry

Form with: full name, email, type (contractor/freelancer/creator), country, phone, tags. Each persona gets a unique sub-code (e.g. ACME-X7K2PF).

📄

CSV bulk upload

Upload a CSV file with columns: full_name, email, type, country, phone. Preview first 5 rows before importing. All validated before insert.

🏷

Tags & types

3 persona types: Contratista (purple), Creator (orange), Freelancer (lime). Custom tags for organization (e.g. "Design", "Engineering").

👥 Personas

Manage your team

Full directory of all personas (workers/freelancers/creators) the company has added. Searchable, filterable, with quick-pay buttons on each row.

🔍

Search & filter

Search by name, email, or sub-code. Filter by type: Contratista, Creator, Freelancer.

💰

Quick Pay

Each persona row has a "Pagar" button that opens an inline payment form. Enter amount, note, send.

🔐

Unique sub-codes

Every persona gets an auto-generated code (e.g. ACME-X7K2PF) for tracking and identification.

💰 Payments & Registration

Pay & grow

The company sends payments to their personas and can share a unique registration link for new workers to self-register.

💳

Payments page

Select multiple personas, set amount in USD, choose one-time or recurring (weekly/bi-weekly/monthly), add a note, send. All payments tracked with status: pending, processing, completed.

🔗

Registration link

Auto-generated company-specific URL (e.g. midi-kyb-portal.netlify.app/join/ACME-2026). Share with workers so they can self-register under the company. Copy link with one click.

📈

Payment history

Full log of all transactions with dates, amounts, recipients, status badges. Searchable and sortable.
